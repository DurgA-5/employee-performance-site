Our Machine Learning Approach

We transform raw data into predictive intelligence through a structured, four-step process designed for accuracy and reliability.

1

Data Integration & Pre-processing

We start by collecting and cleaning diverse data points, including performance metrics, team structure, and task complexity.

2

Predictive Forecasting
